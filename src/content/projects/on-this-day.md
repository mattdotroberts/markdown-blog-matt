A personalised yearbook generator that creates unique content for every day of the year. Upload your memories and let AI craft a beautiful book you can view digitally or print.

## Tech Stack

- **AI**: Google Gemini for content generation
- **Backend**: Cloud Code
- **Database**: Neon (PostgreSQL)

## Features

- **Daily pages** - Generates unique content for each day of the year
- **AI-powered** - Uses Google Gemini to create personalised narratives
- **Digital viewing** - Browse your yearbook online anytime
- **Print ready** - Export and print physical copies of your book
